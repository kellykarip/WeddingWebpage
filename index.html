Practical information Where to go and where to stay

Getting to the island of Ammouliani


If you are comming from outside Greece, the airport you should fly to is Thessaloniki Airport (SKG), called Makedonia. From the Airport it is a two hours drive to Tripiti in Chalkidki and from Tripiti it takes 15 minutes to reach the island and the village of Ammouliani by Ferry. The Ferry takes both people and cars.

Check with the hotel you choose to stay at in Ammouliani if they provide transfer to your hotel from the Airport. If they do we strongly recommend you to book that if you want to smoothly move from the Airport to you hotel. On the other hand, if you want to be able to easily move around on the island and having the possibility of travelling around in the area of Chalkidiki we recommend you to rent a car. If you are thinking of renting a car we highly recommend you to rent it already from the Airport and travel to Ammouliani by car. The Airport is on the right side of Thessaloniki when travelling to Chalkidiki, so you don't need to worry about needing to drive through the city center of Thessaloniki if you don't want to ;)

A map of Thessaloniki and Chalkidiki

The whole area of Chalkidiki is famous for it's many, many amazingly beautiful beaches so if you have time we highly recommend you to take the opportunity to travel to other places in Chalkidiki too either for staying there over night or for daily trips! Our favorite places and beaches, besides Ammouliani, are located on the middle peninsula of Chalkidiki's three peninsulas. The three places we like the most are Neos Marmaras, a really beautiful and quite big village with a nice and really long beach and a lot of restaurants, bars and LIV O RÖRELSE, Kalamitsi a smaller village with definitly one of Chalkidikis nicest beaches and Porto Koufo a small fishing village located in a lagoon, "hidden" from the open sea but with very deep water inside, where the Greek navy historically have been hiding to surprise enemies.
