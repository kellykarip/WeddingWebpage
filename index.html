Practical information Where to go and where to stay

Travel to the island of Ammouliani


If you are coming from outside Greece, the airport you should fly to is Thessaloniki Airport (SKG). From the airport it is a two hours drive (~120 km) to Tripiti in Chalkidki and from Tripiti it takes 15 minutes to reach the island of Ammouliani by ferry. The ferry takes both people and cars and docks at the village of Ammouliani.

It is good to check with the hotel you choose to stay at in Ammouliani if they provide transfer to your hotel from Thessaloniki Airport. If they do it could be nice to book that if you want to smoothly get to your hotel. On the other hand, if you want to be able to easily move around on the island during your vacation and/or having the possibility of travelling around in the area of Chalkidiki we highly recommend you to rent a car. If you are thinking of renting a car it is probably best to rent it already at the airport and travel to Ammouliani by car. The car renting services on the island are limited. The airport is on the south east side of Thessaloniki, so when travelling to Chalkidiki you don't have to worry about driving through the city center of Thessaloniki.

Stay at the island of Ammouliani

The Wedding dinner and party will be at the Agionissi Resort and therefore the absolutely most convinient place to stay at is Agionissi Resort. To book rooms there you can either visit Agionissi's webpage or look for Agionissi Resort at your favorite hotel-booking site.

Agionissi Resort is probably the most expensive alternative on the island, so if you want to stay at another place we recommend the island's, Ammouliani's, own webpage. At the webage you can find suggestions on different hotels but also other information about the island. There are more hotels than the ones mentioned on the island's webpage so it is a smart idea to check your favorite hotel-booking site for even more alternatives.

The ferry to Ammouliani docks at the village of Ammouliani which is where most restaurants, cafés and stores are located on the island. If you prefer to be close to these things you might want to look for a hotel that is located in the village of Ammouliani. Most of the hotels/apartments, at least 20 different, are located in the actual village.

Agionissi Resort is on a 2,6 km distance from the village (and that's the reason why we can party all night long there) so if you would prefer to stay closer to Agionissi Resort it might be smart to check the following hotels out. We have not done any further research on them so we can really not promise anything about how good/bad they are. The only thing we have checked is their distance to Agionissi Resort. Pyrgos rooms is the Resort's closest neighbour only 350 meters away, Liani ammos apartments is 1,1 km from Agionissi Resort, Alikes Sea-front Apartments is located on the most famous beach of Ammouliani, Alikes, and is located 1,5 km from Agionissi Resort and Athos Bay Villa is located 1,7 km from Agionissi Resort. To reach Liani ammos apartments, Alikes Sea-front Apartments and Athos Bay Villa the most convinient way would be to drive because the roads connecting them with Agionissi Resort are proper car roads, but if you bring a pair of comfortable shoes to walk in maybe you can walk the distances.

What does the dress code 'business casual, tie optional' mean?

'Business casual' (called "Kavaj" in swedish) means that you can wear a light colored suite, it doesn't need to be a dark one, since it's a summer wedding in Greece. For the same reason there is no obligation of wearing any tie or bow-tie. If you prefer to wear a dress rather than suit, feel free to wear a cocktail dress or any summer dress you feel confortable with wearing :)

Do you have suggestions on what to do if we want to travel around in the area of Chalkidiki?

The whole area of Chalkidiki is famous for its many, many amazingly beautiful beaches so if you have time and want to explore the area we definitely recommend you to take the opportunity to also travel to other places in Chalkidiki, either for staying overnight or for daily trips! Our favorite places and beaches, besides Ammouliani, are located on the middle peninsula of Chalkidiki's three peninsulas. The three places we like the most are: Neos Marmaras, a really beautiful and quite big village with a nice and really long beach and a lot of restaurants, bars and active nightlife; Kalamitsi, a smaller village with definitly one of Chalkidiki's nicest beaches; Porto Koufo, a small fishing village located in a lagoon, "hidden" from the open sea but with very deep water inside, where the Greek navy historically have been hiding to surprise enemies.
