Practical information Where to go and where to stay

Getting to the island of Ammouliani

If you are comming from outside Greece, the airport you should fly to is Thessaloniki Airport (SKG), called "Makedonia". From the Airport it is a two hours drive to Tripiti in Chalkidki, the place you take the Ferry from to get to Ammouliani island.

Check with the hotel you choose to stay at in Ammouliani if they provide transfer to the hotel from the Airport. If they do we strongly recommend you to book that if you want to smoothly move from the Airport to you hotel. On the other hand, if you want to be able to easily move from place to place in Ammouliani and having the possibility of travel around in the area of Chalkidiki we recommend you to rent a car. If you are thinking of renting a car we highly recommend you to rent it already from the Airport and travel to Ammouliani by car. The Airport is on the right side of the city when travelling to Chalkidiki, so you don't need to worry about needing to drive through the city center of Thessaloniki at all ;)

The whole area of Chalkidiki is famous for it's many, many amazingly beutyful beaches so if you have time we highly recommend you to take the opportunity to travel to other places in Chalkidiki too either for staying there over night or for daily trips! Our favorite places and beaches are Neos Marmaras, Porto Koufo and Kalamitsi all three of them located on the middle peninsula of Chalkidiki's three peninsulas. If you like the idea of exploring Chalkidiki you will definitly want to rent a car!
